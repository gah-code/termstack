## Overview

A content type is a reusable structure that defines the fields, rules, and purpose of a specific kind of content.

In a CMS, content types prevent every page from becoming a custom one-off build.

## Simple example

A blog post content type might include:

- Title
- Slug
- Author
- Publish date
- Hero image
- Body
- SEO title
- Meta description

## Why it matters

Content types help teams create consistent content without rebuilding structure every time.

They also help developers, marketers, and editors speak the same language.
